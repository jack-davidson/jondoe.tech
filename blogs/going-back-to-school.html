Going Back to School
____________________

I am a student attending middle school and I will be doing lessons online. The
technology used by teachers during online school is not always the best and If
there are any teachers reading my blog, consider some of my feedback on school
online.


Zoom is not Great

I don't think that Zoom is the best option for doing lessons online. Some
of my teachers on Blend, a student-teacher class organization web platform, used
BigBlueButton. BigBlueButton is a FOSS video conferencing program designed GNU/Linux
servers and licensed under the LGPL3 license. BigBlueButton is great, has a good
interface, and most importantly, FOSS. BigBlueButton is
